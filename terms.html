Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme selection (light/dark mode)
  • Remembering your booking preferences

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services and user experience.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our site.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, which may place cookies on your device. These services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Refund Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 48 hours before pickup: Full refund (100%)
  • 24-48 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: No refund
  • No-show: No refund

2. How to Cancel

To cancel your booking, please contact us at:

  • Email: bookings@vanitysign.com
  • Phone: +351 900 000 000

Please include your booking reference number in all cancellation requests.

3. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days to the original payment method. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed.

4. Modifications

Changes to your booking (date, time, pickup/drop-off location) can be made free of charge if requested more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Changes within 24 hours may incur additional charges.

5. Service Issues

If you experience any issues with our service, please contact us immediately. We are committed to resolving all complaints fairly and may offer:

  • Partial refund
  • Full refund
  • Credit for future bookings

6. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (extreme weather, natural disasters, strikes, etc.), we will work with you to either reschedule your transfer or provide a full refund.

7. Flight Delays

We monitor flight arrivals. If your flight is delayed, we will adjust your pickup time accordingly at no extra charge. This service applies only when flight information is provided at booking.

8. Disputes

If you are not satisfied with our response to a refund request, you may escalate the matter by contacting our customer service manager at support@vanitysign.com
